Module 6

NLP & Transformers

⏱️ Estimated time: 8-10 hours

🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Understand text preprocessing techniques for NLP
  • Explain word embeddings (Word2Vec, GloVe) and their significance
  • Understand the Transformer architecture and attention mechanisms
  • Use pre-trained models like BERT and GPT for various NLP tasks
  • Implement text classification, summarization, and generation
  • Discuss ethical considerations in NLP and generative AI

📚 Topics Covered

6.1 NLP Fundamentals
6.2 Word Embeddings
6.3 Attention Mechanisms
6.4 The Transformer Architecture
6.5 BERT and Modern NLP
6.6 Generative AI & Ethics

📋 Prerequisites

Completion of Modules 1-5. Understanding of deep learning and neural networks. Familiarity with sequence models from Module 5 is helpful.
