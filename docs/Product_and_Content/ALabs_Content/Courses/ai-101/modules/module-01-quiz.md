## Question 1
**Which of the following best describes Artificial Intelligence?**

A) The ability of computers to perform calculations faster than humans  
B) The field of computer science focused on creating machines that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence  
C) A specific programming language for robotics  
D) Only deep learning and neural networks  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 2
**True or False: All machine learning is AI, but not all AI uses machine learning.**

A) True  
B) False  

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:** Traditional AI used hard-coded rules (expert systems), while ML learns patterns from data. ML is a subset of AI.

---

## Question 3
**Who coined the term "Artificial Intelligence"?**

A) Alan Turing  
B) John McCarthy  
C) Marvin Minsky  
D) Geoffrey Hinton  

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:** John McCarthy coined the term at the Dartmouth Workshop in 1956.

---

## Question 4
**Which type of AI exists today?**

A) Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)  
B) Artificial Superintelligence (ASI)  
C) Artificial Narrow Intelligence (ANI)  
D) Both A and B  

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:** Only ANI (narrow AI designed for specific tasks) exists today. AGI and ASI remain theoretical.

---

## Question 5
**In supervised learning, the algorithm learns from:**

A) Trial and error with rewards  
B) Finding patterns in unlabeled data  
C) Labeled examples with known inputs and outputs  
D) Random exploration of the environment  

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:** Supervised learning uses labeled training data where the correct output is provided for each input.

---

## Question 6
**Which learning approach would be most appropriate for training an AI to play chess?**

A) Supervised learning with labeled game positions  
B) Unsupervised learning to cluster similar positions  
C) Reinforcement learning through gameplay  
D) None of the above  

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:** Reinforcement learning learns through trial and error with rewards (winning) and penalties (losing), which is ideal for game playing.

---

## Question 7
**The "AI Winter" periods were characterized by:**

A) Rapid advances in deep learning  
B) Reduced funding and interest due to unmet expectations  
C) The invention of the first neural networks  
D) AI systems achieving human-level intelligence  

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:** AI Winters occurred when early promises failed to materialize, leading to reduced funding and skepticism.

---

## Question 8
**Deep Learning is:**

A) The same as machine learning  
B) A subset of machine learning using neural networks with multiple layers  
C) A completely separate field from AI  
D) Only used for image recognition  

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:** Deep learning is a subset of ML that uses neural networks with multiple (deep) layers to learn complex patterns.

---

## Question 9
**An example of unsupervised learning is:**

A) Email spam detection with labeled emails  
B) Grouping customers based on purchasing behavior without predefined categories  
C) Training a robot to walk using reward signals  
D) Predicting house prices from historical sales data  

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:** Customer segmentation without predefined labels is a classic unsupervised learning task (clustering).

---

## Question 10
**Which three factors have driven recent advances in AI?**

A) Faster internet, social media, and mobile devices  
B) Massive datasets, powerful hardware (GPUs), and better algorithms  
C) Quantum computing, blockchain, and virtual reality  
D) Government regulations, academic research, and open-source software  

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:** The combination of big data, GPU computing power, and algorithmic innovations (like Transformers) has enabled modern AI breakthroughs.
