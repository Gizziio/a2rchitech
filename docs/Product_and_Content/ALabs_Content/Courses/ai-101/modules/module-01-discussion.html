Module 1 Discussion

💬 Introductions: Your AI Journey

Discussion Prompt

Welcome to Introduction to AI & Machine Learning! We're a diverse community of learners with different backgrounds and goals. Please introduce yourself to the class and share your thoughts on AI.

Please include in your response:

1. About You

  • Your name and background (professional or academic)
  • Your location/time zone
  • Your experience with programming and data

2. Your AI Interests

  • What sparked your interest in AI/ML?
  • What do you hope to achieve by taking this course?
  • Are you interested in a specific AI application (NLP, computer vision, robotics, etc.)?

3. AI in Your Life

  • What AI tools or applications do you use regularly?
  • Have you had any surprising or concerning experiences with AI?
  • What excites you most (or worries you most) about AI's future?

4. Your Learning Goals

  • What specific skills do you want to develop?
  • Do you have a project in mind you'd like to build?
  • How do you plan to apply what you learn?
📝 Guidelines:
  • Write at least 150 words to receive full participation credit
  • Respond to at least 2 classmates' posts to build community
  • Be respectful and supportive—we're all here to learn together
  • Feel free to ask questions about the course or AI in general
💡 Discussion Tips:
  • Use specific examples when describing your AI experiences
  • Ask follow-up questions when replying to classmates
  • Share resources or articles you've found helpful
  • Connect with classmates who have similar interests
