Module 4

Neural Networks

⏱️ Estimated time: 7-8 hours

🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Explain the biological inspiration behind neural networks
  • Implement a perceptron and understand its limitations
  • Build a multi-layer neural network from scratch
  • Understand activation functions and their purposes
  • Implement backpropagation for training networks
  • Use TensorFlow/Keras to build neural networks efficiently

📚 Topics Covered

4.1 Biological Inspiration
4.2 The Perceptron
4.3 Multi-Layer Networks
4.4 Backpropagation
4.5 Activation Functions
4.6 TensorFlow & Keras

📋 Prerequisites

Completion of Modules 1-3. Comfort with Python, NumPy, and matrix operations. Basic calculus understanding (derivatives) is essential for backpropagation.
