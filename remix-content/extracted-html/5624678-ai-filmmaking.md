## About this module section

*Chapter: 6-narrative-theory | Words: 109*

Narrative is as important in the context of AI filmmaking as it is with filmmaking in general. Additionally, there are special considerations related to creating narrative flows out of material that will feature more of a montage logic, given that a large collection of AI-generated clips may not have as much visual continuity between them as with regular cinematic production. This section contains a curated selected of modules from my free Udemy course Narrative in Linear and Interactive Media . If the modules in this section interest you, consider enrolling in that course as well since it covers more topics related to interactive storytelling and other aspects of narrative.
